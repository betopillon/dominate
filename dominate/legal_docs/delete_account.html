Account Deletion Request

We're sorry to see you go! If you would like to delete your Dominate account and all associated data, please follow the process below.

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your game statistics, profile information, and leaderboard entries will be permanently removed.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your user profile and account information
  • All game statistics and progress
  • Leaderboard entries and rankings
  • Any stored preferences or settings
  • Authentication credentials

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account, please send an email with the following information:

Email: pillonnnnn@gmail.com
Subject: Account Deletion Request - Dominate App
Include:
  • Your registered email address
  • Your username/nickname (if you remember it)
  • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been permanently deleted.

Alternative: In-App Deletion

You can also delete your account directly from within the Dominate app:

  • Open the Dominate app
  • Go to Profile/Settings
  • Look for "Delete Account" option
  • Follow the confirmation steps

Questions or Issues?

If you have any questions about the account deletion process or encounter any issues, please contact us at pillonnnnn@gmail.com.
